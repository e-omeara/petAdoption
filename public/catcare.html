How to care for your cyber cat!!

For a long and fruitful companionship

Digital cats, like their flesh-and-blood counterparts, require love and care to live long, healthy lives. You will want to host them in a nice comfortable server where they have plenty of room to run around and explore. You must play with them daily, and ensure that they have much to keep them entertained.
Digicats need fresh food and water daily. A high-quality diet of HTML and CSS is critical to keeping your cat healthy. Your cat also needs brushing. It is best to id your pet so that they do not get lost or pirated. If you let your cat roam around other websites, be sure to vaccinate them against viruses or other malware.
